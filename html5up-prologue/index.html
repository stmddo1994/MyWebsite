About Me

During my years from elementary to high school, I had not quite considered a career in technology or computer-based work, but I had always been very close with tech. I've had a knack for working computers to the point around middle school, I could help install and fix some operational issues for other family members. I also always had a knack for math, but I started a career path for accounting. I graduated from high school with a dual enrollment from College Academy at Broward College. Afterward, I enlisted into the Air Force, serving for six years. During my time, I heavily considered looking further into the workings of technology and even began ROM hacking a video game for fun. After separating from the Air Force, I attended Nova Southeastern University and completed the Bachelor's Degree program, learning code for Python, C++, Java, SQL, and other programs. I also made a couple video game demonstrations on Unity.
